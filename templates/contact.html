Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar. You'll need to provide a username, email address, and password. You can sign up as either a job seeker or an employer.

First, you need to create an employer account. Once logged in, you'll have access to your employer dashboard. From there, click on "Post a Job" and fill out the job posting form with all the necessary details.

Browse our job listings and click on any job you're interested in to view details. On the job page, click the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to upload your resume and optionally include a cover letter with your application.

After logging in, access your dashboard (either candidate or employer). Click on "Edit Profile" to update your information, add skills or experience, or upload a new resume.
